Everything before us brings us to today. We become good at something through continuous new experiences, facing challenges, overcoming obstacles, exploring and evaluating, learning from our mistakes and those of others, evolving.

These experiences increase our knowledge, though knowledge has no value unless it is utilized.... and wisdom belongs to one person unless it is shared.

Sharing wisdom with those who do not have your knowledge is difficult though can be achieved if you can relate to them on a personal level, using language they understand, and linking your messages to their own experiences, fears, hopes and dreams.

The COACH Approach® is not an invention, it has evolved over two decades in Asian environments. The Fundamental 3s have evolved to be the foundation of COACH Collaborations which transform organizations by transforming the people within them.

Below is a simple configuration which clearly demonstrates that there is something for everyone at every level in a COACH Collaboration, for the groups or teams they are part of, the organization as a whole and I dare say the outside community too.

The Fundamental 3s in detail are available to those interested in collaborating with COACH*. Inquiries are welcomed.
