The Coach Approach®

The COACH Approach® has evolved over two decades of applying international management concepts and methodologies in more than twelve countries in Asia to achieve measurable and sustainable improvements in Asian environments.

This experience resulted in an approach and implementation model that is more effective and more appropriate than a force-fed Western implementation style, in that it recognizes, honors and utilizes local custom and culture.

Your most asked questions are answered below.....



Why should I be interested in COACH?

COACH has a simple approach to harnessing the causes of internal losses, and converting them into high value activity, or into cash.

COACH specializes in business simplification. Simplification of management processess, simplification of communication between departments and between individuals, simplification of planning and performance review forums.

How does COACH do it?

COACH doesn't do it. We do it together... It's the only way.

COACH works with you to change the mindset within your organization, redirecting and re-focusing those attributes and activities which really contribute to achieving and exceeding your company objectives.

COACH systematically improves attitudes, opinions, knowledge, skill and judgment of your personnel, resulting in better work habits and a pro-active approach to problem-solving and opportunity-taking. Communication and cooperation will visibly improve.

COACH works with you to re-assign your resources to more closely match your actual output. This delivers a more consistent performance and increased predictability, enabling accurate planning, and much more control over your operation.

...... and all the time we are transferring knowledge and COACHING your staff to take over and drive the improvements themselves!

..... and the outcome?

These and many other techniques collectively result in many, many improvements that fall to the bottom line as additional profit or better OUTCOMES. You can expect 'across the board' improvements in your input/ourput ratios.

Why COACH?

At COACH, we keep it simple. We don't mess with your processes. We improve what can be improved without interfering. We don't need to see your balance sheets, formulas or other sensitive information.

But my business is different!

Every COACH Collaboration is different too. Yours will be tailored to YOUR situation, in YOUR environment, focusing on YOUR needs. Together we will elevate current performance within YOUR Organization to its next natural level.

WHAT's the DEAL?

Every COACH business proposition is simple and attractive, in that is based on a fair formula of shared risk and shared gain. We work together, we win together.

.....and your COACH business proposition is very flexible, to suit whatever your own situation and/or needs are.

Your program will be structured to pay for itself relatively quickly, and you can reasonably expect to profit for years.

Sounds good, how do we proceed?

Winning starts with beginning. The next step is to agree areas of common interest, areas of oppurtunity, agree on the scope of our collaboration, and make a start.

If you feel there is room for improvement within your organization, and want substantial improvements across your various input/output ratios, you should seriously consider a COACH Collaboration now.
