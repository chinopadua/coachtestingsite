About
Founder

JIM NICHOLLS - Chairman/Founder



I am a New Zealander, my history is in Process Re-engineering and Project Management, and I have lived and worked in Asia/South-East Asia since 1997. Over these many years I have developed an approach and implementing model that recognizes honors and utilizes local custom and culture. I call it the COACH Approach®.

This has proven to be far more successful, and I might say more appropriate, than a force-fed Western implementation style. It wasn’t easy, my approach evolved through facing the challenging situations of myself and clients, learning from my mistakes, and those of others, to achieve measurable and sustainable improvements in Asian environments.

I previously held almost every leadership position in Asia for an American-based company which specializes in productivity improvement via process re-engineering. On that journey my titles included 'Project Director', 'Unit Vice-President' (UVP, overseeing multiple projects), Head of Executive Training (for all of Asia), ACCESS Manager (responsible for organizational behavioral change in all Asian projects), VP of Sales–Asia and ultimately “Chairman-Asia‘.

I have more than 25 years in management. Two decades of process re-engineering, project management and applying the COACH Approach® in Asia, New Zealand and Australia. I have implemented these programs in more than 12 Asian countries, and have worked with close to 400 business leaders and their decision-groups.
