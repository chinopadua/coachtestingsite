Pepsi Cola
A Message from the CEO

I am happy to share my COACH experience with other business leaders At PEPSI we are proud of the policies, processes, systems and procedures we have developed o ensure consistently high standards in our products and services.

However, we were caught our last summer by an unusually long, hot & dry season that put pressure on our capacity and tested our individual and organizational capabilities. Whilst we maintained our quality standards, we struggled to maintain our service standards, and at times our supply to retail outlets. Our efforts to correct the situation were frustrated by many small problems occurring in our production facilities.

We as a management group determined that we could and should have done better and chose to engage COACH to conduct a thorough diagnostic exercise of our operation.

Findings were credible in that the COACH crew worked long hours, covering all days of the week, and compliance to them, to be ‘improvable’ For example, whilst our hiring criteria is demanding for new hires, we were found to not have invested enough time and energy in upgrading the knowledge and skills of our existing ‘long service’ staff to match those standards.

Opportunities for saving in energy, chemicals, machine and labor utilization were statistically proven, whilst observational studies identified the cause of significant ‘Lost Time’. PEPSI has already benefited from complete list of ‘Problems by Cause’ and ‘Solutions by Category’ provided by COACH, as many of these we can implement by ourselves. I will also though be happy to engage COACH again to help us realize our full potential.

Perhaps the best endorsement I can give is that, should we be faced with another long hot & dry summer, I am confident that we will maintain both continuous and timely supply of quality product to the PEPSI drinking public. I suggest to any business leader who wants to improve consistency, and improve profits, in his/her organization, that a conversation with COACH will be time-well spent Yours sincerely

Partho Chakrabarti
CEO, Pepsi Cola Products Philippines, Inc
