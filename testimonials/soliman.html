A Message from the GM

Our company, Soliman Security Services, Inc. has been doing business in the field of security for the past 57 years. Modesty aside, I would say that we belong to the top 10 security agencies in the Philippines. We had a very simple beginning as a company and slowly but steadily our number multiplied not only a hundred times but in leaps and bounds. Our workforce today is close to 4000.

What we are right now is the product of my pure dedication and strive for perfection; my eyes for details; my commitments to deliver the security services as our clients would want it and even surpassing their expectations.

The continuous search for improvement has compelled me to venture into engaging the collaborative services of COACH Business Simplification, Inc. At first, there was a mixed of emotions and anxiety as to the outcome of the project.

At the end of the collaboration, there were noticeable changes in our systems and processes of doing things. Among the tangible fruits of the COACH Collaborations program is the introduction of the tracking tools like tardiness, billing, collection and most important of all is the monitoring of guards who are on floating status. The simplification of daily time record encoding with a simultaneous output of invoicing is one big success. The control measures on overtime has lowered the expense on the company. We do not only look on the output as a success in expenses control but also on the aspect of our staff having more time for themselves and their family.

In summary, the COACH Collaboration program has introduced innovation and simplification of our process which were done in such a fashion that the staff are not threatened but excited about changes. The programs produced tangible and intangible results which is to the best interest of the company and the staff.

Thank you.

Very truly yours,

Teresita L. Soliman
General Manager
